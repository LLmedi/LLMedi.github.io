Rubber Mayhem - 2023

Every tree, sign and building is destructible. As a bouncy rubber ball, the player must smash into objects to collect as many points possible before the timer runs out.

As this was a solo project, I implemented all gameplay mechanics, including a custom character controller, collision detection, destructible environment, upgrade system, saving and loading, and UI.

The Dreamrooms VR - 2023

The backrooms are known for an immersive world and its sense of isolation. As VR puts a player inside the world, this seemed like a fun combination to explore.

In Unreal Engine 5, I recreated the classic backrooms level and began working on custom VR controls. I implemented smooth full VR locomotion and custom hand animations.

I now currently work on this project for an upcoming game jam this month.

Ghost Run - 2020

During my time in University, I reached out to a few artists and programmers to form a team for this project. As the most experienced, I helped my teammates become familiar with Unity.

I implemented collision detection, player life/death mechanic, randomized enemy and platform spawn, and background parallax scrolling

Unfortunately, development stopped due to COVID and the project remained unfinished.
